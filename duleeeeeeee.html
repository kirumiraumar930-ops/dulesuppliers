What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive industrial maintenance and repair services for refrigeration, compressed air, and electrical systems.

Industrial Refrigeration

Full servicing and repair of industrial chillers, cold rooms, and refrigeration systems including Weco, Danfoss, and Carel controlled units.

Screw Compressors

Maintenance, fault diagnosis, and repair of Kaishan, Ingersoll Rand, Shangair, and ELGi screw compressors for industrial applications.

Compressed Air Systems

Installation, servicing, and maintenance of complete compressed air systems including dryers, filters, and distribution pipework.

Electrical Services

Industrial electrical installation, panel wiring, motor control systems, and fault finding for manufacturing and processing facilities.

Gensets & Generators

Supply, installation, servicing, and repair of industrial gensets and generators including Kirloskar units with DSE controller diagnostics and configuration.

Boilers

Installation, servicing, and maintenance of industrial boilers for manufacturing and processing facilities across Uganda.

Compressor Oils & Parts

Supply of genuine compressor oils, filters, and spare parts through our sister company Mumbai Trading Uganda Limited.

About Us

Who We Are

Dule Suppliers (U) Ltd is a Kampala-based industrial servicing company specialising in refrigeration systems, compressed air solutions, and electrical services for manufacturing and processing industries in Uganda.

We work with some of Uganda's leading manufacturers, providing preventive maintenance contracts, emergency breakdown support, and major overhaul projects. Our technical team has hands-on experience with leading brands including Kaishan, Ingersoll Rand, Weco, Danfoss, Carel, Grundfos, and ELGi.

Our sister company, Mumbai Trading Uganda Limited, ensures a reliable local supply of compressor oils, filters, and spare parts.
